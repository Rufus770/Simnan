FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We accept DXF, AI, CDR, SVG, PDF for cutting and routing. For engraving, we also accept JPG, PNG, and BMP image files.

What is the minimum order quantity?

We have no minimum order — we happily work on single prototype pieces as well as large-batch production runs. Pricing scales accordingly.

How long does delivery take?

Standard turnaround is 1–3 business days for most orders. Complex or large-volume orders may take 4–7 days. We always confirm timelines upfront.

Do you offer material supply as well?

Yes! We can source most materials — acrylic, MDF, wood, PVC — or you can supply your own material and we'll cut to your specifications.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely. We accept client-supplied designs in all standard formats. Our team reviews them for cutting feasibility before production.

Do you ship outside Hubli?

Yes, we dispatch products via courier to locations across Karnataka and other states. Shipping costs are calculated based on weight and destination.
